User Data Deletion

Effective date: July 6, 2026

You may request deletion of personal data associated with your Atlas Deep AI account or with data received through connected integrations (including Meta Messenger and Instagram).

How to request deletion

  1. Email support@atlasdeepai.com from the address associated with your account.
  2. Include your company name, account email, and whether you want full account deletion or deletion of specific connected integration data.
  3. We will verify your identity and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

What we delete

Upon a verified request, we will delete or anonymize applicable account data, stored credentials references, and associated execution logs, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate security purposes.

Meta-connected data

If you connected Meta services, you may also revoke access in Meta account settings. After revocation, we will delete stored credential secrets and stop receiving new webhook events for that connection.
