Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Atlas Deep AI ("we", "us", or "our") provides a B2B workflow automation platform that helps businesses connect third-party services—including Meta (Facebook Messenger and Instagram)—to automate messaging and related workflows.

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to users of the Atlas Deep AI platform, including company administrators, team members, and individuals who interact with our customers through connected channels (for example, by sending a message to a business Page or Instagram professional account that uses our platform).

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the platform, we may process:

  • Account information: name, email address, company affiliation, and authentication identifiers from our identity provider.
  • Connected service credentials: OAuth tokens, API keys, and related metadata required to connect Meta, Google, Microsoft, and other providers. Secret values are stored in encrypted secret storage; we do not expose raw tokens in the application UI.
  • Messaging and webhook data: when you connect Meta Messenger or Instagram, we may receive webhook payloads such as sender identifiers, recipient identifiers, message text, timestamps, and message IDs needed to run workflows and send replies on your behalf.
  • Workflow and execution data: workflow definitions, execution logs, and operational metadata required to run and audit automations.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and diagnostic logs for security and reliability.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Authenticate users and enforce company-level access controls;
  • Connect and operate integrations you authorize;
  • Receive inbound messages via webhooks and execute workflows you configure;
  • Send outbound messages when your workflows request it;
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve platform reliability;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell personal information.

4. Meta (Facebook / Instagram) data

When you connect Meta services:

  • We access only the data and permissions you grant through Meta authorization or credentials you provide.
  • Webhook events are used to trigger workflows and may be stored in execution logs according to your company's configuration.
  • We use Meta app credentials (such as app secret and webhook verify token) solely to validate webhook requests and operate the integration securely.

Your use of Meta services is also subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.

5. How we share information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the platform (for example, cloud hosting, secret management, email delivery, and error monitoring), under contractual confidentiality obligations;
  • Connected platforms (such as Meta) when required to send or receive messages you authorize;
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

6. Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Company administrators may request deletion as described in our Data Deletion page.

7. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate to a B2B SaaS platform, including encrypted credential storage, access controls, and audit logging. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@atlasdeepai.com.

9. Children

Our platform is intended for business use and is not directed to children under 13.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
